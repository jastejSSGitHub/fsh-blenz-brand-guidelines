      01 — The Shift From quiet to confident · Type-first

      Coffee is the heritage.
      The wordmark is the future.

      For thirty years Blenz spoke softly. The new identity speaks plainly. One wordmark. Two cup colourways. A copy device borrowed from Italian café culture. Less to maintain, more to remember.

      / Was — 1992 to 2026

      Polite. Quiet. A little beige.

      Honey-yellow honesty. Frutiger uppercase. Rose-windmill icon. A brand that whispered "established" instead of saying anything memorable.

      • BLENZ COFFEE — formal caps
      • Pantone 1215 honey
      • Watercolour mascot, used rarely
      • Coffee-only positioning
      / Is — 2026 onward

      Bold. Disciplined. Yours.

      Lowercase wordmark in a contemporary grotesque. Two cup colourways — yellow for everyday, crimson for cold. Bracket copy as brand punctuation. Built to scale to a hundred locations.

      02 — Personality Four principles · One signature

      Confident, never loud.
      Warm, never soft.

      Blenz holds its space with type and colour, not noise. The four principles below define what the brand is — and just as importantly, what it refuses to be.

      01

      Disciplined

      Two colours do most of the work. One wordmark fits every surface. Restraint is the brand.

      02

      Warm

      A regular's "good morning," not a server's script. The type is sharp; the people behind it are not.

      03

      Local

      Vancouver-built. BC-rooted. Made for Canadian morning light, exported with that origin intact.

      04

      Made

      Real ingredients. Real latte art. Real hands. The drink is the proof; the brand sets the table.

      10 — Seasonal Cups Limited drops · Type stays · Characters rotate

      The cup changes.
      The wordmark doesn't.

      Blenz has an existing seasonal cup tradition — illustrated characters at the holidays, collectible and shareable. In Direction B this system is kept deliberately quiet. The wordmark holds its position. A single character illustration occupies the lower cup wall. The cup colourway switches to crimson for the seasonal drop. One image prompt covers this section.

      [ IMG-SEASONAL ]

      Two blenz cups side by side — one yellow (everyday), one crimson (seasonal limited). The crimson cup has a bold flat-vector BC moose illustration in bone and yellow below the wordmark. Editorial top-lit studio shot, both cups casting soft shadows on a bone surface. The wordmark reads clearly on both.

      01

      Seasonal = crimson cup

      All limited and seasonal drops use the crimson cup. Yellow stays for the everyday menu. The colour swap signals it's special without any copy.

      02

      Wordmark position never moves

      The blenz wordmark sits in the same fixed position on every cup — seasonal or not. The character lives in the lower 40% only.

      03

      BC-rooted characters, two colours only

      Bone and yellow on crimson. Characters are from BC's natural world — orca, moose, grizzly, heron. Flat vector, no outlines, shapes only.

      04

      Four drops per year

      Holiday, Spring, Summer, Autumn. Each cup is a limited collectible. The limited colourway and character make it worth keeping.

      11 — Voice Specific · Brief · Bracketed

      Specific. Brief. Local.
      In brackets when it counts.

      Blenz writes the way a regular speaks. Short sentences. Strong verbs. Real ingredients. Real places. The bracket-copy device — borrowed from Italian café culture — is reserved for taglines and signage moments.

      15 — Motion Disciplined · Confident · Unhurried

      Movement that feels
      composed, never frantic.

      Motion follows the same logic as the type — confident, unhurried, never showy. The wordmark settles, never bounces. Brackets snap into place. Five rules, applied everywhere.

      M·01 Composed Ease cubic-bezier(0.32, 0.72, 0, 1) — primary easing curve. Used for nav, hover, page transitions.
      M·02 Wordmark Settle Opacity 0 → 1 with 6px upward drift. 480ms. The wordmark settles, never bounces.
      M·03 Bracket Snap Parens slide in from outside, copy fades in centre. 320ms total. Used for tagline reveals.
      M·04 Pour Animation For drink visualisations only. Liquid fills cup vertically, 600ms ease-in-out, single overshoot.
      M·05 Sticker Pop Scale 0.85 → 1.0 with subtle rotation jitter ±2°. Used only on loyalty stamp moments.
