      01 — The Shift Story · Repositioning · Vision

      From a coffee shop to a beverage brand.

      Blenz has always been about handcraft. About the maker's mark. About community. That part doesn't change.

      What changes is how we talk to the people drinking. For thirty-three years, the brand spoke in a language built for coffee enthusiasts in 2007. But the people walking up to the counter today aren't just ordering coffee — they're ordering matcha, blue lattes, protein shakes, fruit chillos, cold brews, and drinks that haven't been invented yet.

      Blenz 2.0 is a brand system designed to flex with that menu. A system where coffee is the heritage, but beverages — plural, expressive, colourful — are the future. A system where the brand pops on a phone screen, on a fridge magnet, on a hoodie, and at the back of a drive-thru line.

      This is not a rebrand. It's a confidence upgrade.

      Where Blenz Was

      Premium. Formal. Slightly clinical. Coffee-first. Built for the millennial commuter.

      →

      Where Blenz Is Going

      Bright. Expressive. Personality-driven. Beverage-first. Built for the next generation of regulars.

      02 — Personality Brand traits · Voice · Tone

      Five traits. One Blenz.

      Every piece of communication should feel like one of these traits is leading the room. If a design doesn't read as at least one — it isn't on brand yet.

      01 Bright

      Optimistic, sun-forward, energetic. The yellow is the mood. We are a brand that arrives smiling.

      02 Crafted

      Made by hand, not by machine. Latte art. Single-origin chocolate. Every drink has a maker.

      03 Playful

      Stickers. Stamps. Lowercase wordmarks. We don't take ourselves too seriously, because beverages are joy.

      04 Local

      Proudly Canadian. Vancouver-born. Community-rooted. We belong to the streets we live on.

      05 Open

      For everyone. Not just for coffee people. Matcha, mocha, mango, matterhorn — all welcome.

      07 — Graphic System Stars · Stickers · Stamps

      Three devices. Endless combinations.

      A small kit of repeatable graphic devices that make every Blenz surface unmistakably Blenz. Use them like punctuation — sparingly, with intent, and always in the brand palette.

      b

      The Star Mark

      Four-point starburst surrounds the wordmark or stands alone as a charm. Used for new launches, seasonal drops, and "fresh today" callouts. Always rust on yellow, or yellow on navy.

      smile after every sip

      The Sticker

      A round sticker, slightly tilted, hand-feeling. Used as the monthly drop ritual and as the unit of the loyalty card. Every drink is a chance to give one.

      The Heritage Badge

      A pill-shaped stamp for "since 1992," "made in BC," "by hand." Quiet authority. Used on packaging seams, cup bands, and tote tags.

      09 — Cuppy 2.0 Mascot · Modernised

      Cuppy stays. The watercolour goes.

      Cuppy is one of the most ownable things Blenz has. The 2.0 version is redrawn in a confident vector style — same warmth, less 2017. He lives on stickers, app micro-moments, sticker-card spots, and the occasional in-store standee.

      [IMG-06] Cuppy 2.0 — clean vector illustration of the yellow coffee cup mascot, simplified line work, friendly smile, optional small star ✦ floating beside him. White background.
      • R · 01 Cuppy is always Blenz Yellow with rust accents. No off-palette colour variants.
      • R · 02 Cuppy is a sidekick to the wordmark, never a replacement. He earns the spot, he doesn't claim it.
      • R · 03 Cuppy can hold things — drinks, hearts, sparkles, signs — but never weapons, never sarcasm, never irony.
      • R · 04 Cuppy works best small. Sticker scale, app icon scale, badge scale. He's a charm, not a billboard.
      • R · 05 Cuppy never speaks first-person ("I love you!"). He waves. He smiles. He stays charming.
      10 — Voice Tone · Pillars · Examples

      Warm. Specific. Never corporate.

      Blenz writes like a friend who happens to make really good drinks. We're confident without being cocky. Local without being parochial. Joyful without being saccharine.

      Pillar 01

      Specific

      Real ingredients. Real places. Real numbers. "Single-origin Colombian chocolate" beats "premium ingredients."

      Pillar 02

      Warm

      We greet, we appreciate, we genuinely care. Never transactional. Always a person on the other side of the cup.

      Pillar 03

      Brief

      Cut the corporate fat. Short sentences. Strong verbs. "Smile after every sip" beats "Bringing joy to your daily ritual."

      Pillar 04

      Local

      Vancouver-born, Canadian-proud, neighbourhood-rooted. We know the streets we live on by name.

      How the Sticker Card works

      Step 01

      Pick up a card

      Free at every Blenz counter. No app required, no email required. Just ask.

      Step 02

      Earn a sticker

      Every drink, any size, any kind. Coffee, matcha, blue latte — all count. Barista hands you the sticker with the cup.

      Step 03

      Collect seven

      Stick them on the seven dotted spots. Fill the card. Each month's sticker is a different design — collect them all.

      Step 04

      Eighth on us

      Bring the full card. Pick any drink. The eighth is on the house. Then pick up a new card and start again.

      14 — Motion Animation · Transitions · Feel

      Slow in. Quick out.

      Motion in Blenz 2.0 is warm, unrushed, and confident. Things ease in like a coffee being placed on a counter. Nothing snaps, nothing bounces aggressively.

      Pour-In

      600ms · cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)

      Page sections rise 24px and fade in. Used for hero loads, section reveals, and any first-impression moment.

      Sip Hover

      200ms · ease-out

      Buttons and cards lift 2px, brighten by 4%. Subtle. Confident. Never bouncy.

      Sticker Pop

      320ms · cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)

      Reserved for moments of celebration — sticker added, reward unlocked, order confirmed. The only place a slight overshoot is allowed.

      Yellow Wash

      800ms · ease-in-out

      Page transitions cover with a Blenz Yellow sheet, then reveal the new page. The brand is the transition.
