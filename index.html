      01 — The Shift From quiet to confident · Beverage-first

      Coffee is the heritage.
      Beverages are the future.

      Blenz started with coffee. Thirty years later, we're showing up for every drink — matcha, cold brew, blue lattes, brizas, chillos, and whatever comes next. Same roots. New visual energy. No longer simply "coffee-first"; this brand refresh reflects our bold direction. We are old friends with a new style. A flexible store that shifts throughout the day. Coffee in the morning, variety at night. Be yourself at Blenz.

      We are breaking with formality. BLENZ is now blenz. Scaled back, down to earth. We are a handshake and a hug, not a contract and a signature. More "come hang with us", less "come do your homework here". Less "grab and go coffee" and more "what are you doing after work?" vibe.

      Built to be held. Crafted to be photographed. Designed to feed your soul. Equipped to hit what you crave. Wake up with a latte. Energize mid-day with a chillo. Hang out late at night with a briza. Every craving, designed and crafted for you. By you. Be you.

      Was — 1992 to 2026

      Polite. Quiet. A little beige.

      Honey-yellow honesty. Frutiger uppercase. Rose-windmill icon. Read as warm but a little tired. Built to whisper "established," not to make anyone smile.

      • Muted Pantone 1215 honey
      • Watercolour mascot, used rarely
      • "Coffee" as the only category
      Is — 2026 onward

      Loud. Lowercase. Yours.

      A bolder yellow that pops on a phone screen. A lowercase wordmark that feels like a friend, not a sign. A flexible cup colour system. A mascot you actually want on your jacket.

      02 — Personality Four principles · One voice

      A friend you trust to
      hit that craving you need.

      Blenz isn't trying to be cool. It's trying to be the spot you actually go to. The four principles below are how we behave on a cup, in a caption, on a sign, and at the till.

      01

      Warm, not cold

      A regular's "good morning/good afternoon/good evening," not a server's script. We use names. We ask the small questions.

      02

      Bold, not loud

      Confident colour and type. No exclamation marks. The drink is the headline; the welcome is the wow.

      03

      Local, not regional

      Heritage. Roots. History. Subtlety honors our roots, even when the brand travels to Toronto, Singapore, Dubai.

      04

      Made, not manufactured

      Real ingredients. Real latte art. Real people behind the bar. Visibly human, deliberately handmade.

      04 — Logo Usage Do · Don't · No exceptions

      Treat the wordmark like a signature. Don't trace it twice.

      Do
      Set it big. Let it breathe. Keep tracking tight.
      Don't
      blenz
      No italic. The wordmark is upright. Always.
      Do
      The rust wordmark is allowed at hero scale. It's part of the personality.
      Don't
      BLENZ
      Never set the wordmark in uppercase. Lowercase is the brand.
      Do
      The b. mark is the alternative expression. Use it for app icons, stickers, seasonal cups, badges, and casual touchpoints.
      Don't
      b
      Never use the b. in a script or italic style. The mark is always upright and bold.

      11 — Voice

      Specific. Brief. Local.
      Always present-tense.

      Blenz writes the way a regular speaks. Short sentences. Strong verbs. Real ingredients. Real places. No wellness-speak, no "elevating your daily ritual," no exclamation marks doing the heavy lifting.

      This

      "Made by hand. Crafted by you. Made for the moment you didn't expect to enjoy."

      Not this

      "Elevating your daily ritual through premium handcrafted experiences."

      This

      "Cold brew, slow brewed. Twelve hours, no shortcuts."

      Not this

      "Discover our artisanal cold brew journey, where time meets craft."

      This

      "Smile after every sip."

      Not this

      "Where flavour meets joy in every moment."

      16 — Motion Soft easing · Confident timing

      Movement that feels
      warm, never frantic.

      Motion follows the same logic as the type — confident, unhurried, never showy. Easing is custom-tuned to feel like a slow exhale. Six rules, applied everywhere.

      M·01 Soft Ease cubic-bezier(0.32, 0.72, 0, 1) — primary easing curve. Used for nav, hover, page transitions.
      M·02 Character Bounce 220ms ease-out + 80ms settle. Used when seasonal characters animate (sticker reveal, cup drop).
      M·03 Wordmark Settle Opacity 0 → 1 with subtle 8px upward drift. 480ms. Used on hero load and section reveals.
      M·04 Pour Animation For drink visualisations only. Liquid fills cup vertically, 600ms ease-in-out, slight overshoot.
      M·05 Sticker Pop Scale 0.8 → 1.05 → 1.0 with rotation jitter ±2°. Used on loyalty stamp moments.
      M·06 Stripe Slide Background-position scroll on stripe pattern, infinite loop, 12s linear. Subtle, never distracting.
